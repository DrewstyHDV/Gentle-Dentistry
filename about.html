Dr. Christine Hoang

Dr. Christine Hoang specializes in providing preventative, restorative and cosmetic dental procedures to patients in Lawrence, KS and surrounding areas. Dr. Hoang earned a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Maryland. She then continued her education at the University of Missouri-Kansas City where she graduated with a Doctorate of Dental Surgery degree in May of 1997. She practiced in Overland Park, KS from 1997-2002 before opening her private practice in Lawrence in 2003. Dr. Hoang is a member of the American Dental Association and the Kansas Dental Association. In her spare time, she enjoys spending time with her daughter Audrey.

Gentle Dentistry is a friendly and family-oriented dental practice where you will be cared for and listened to. Dr. Christine Hoang believes in personal relationships with her patients and always strives to provide exactly the dental treatments you need. We hope this website will help answer many of your questions about our office.
